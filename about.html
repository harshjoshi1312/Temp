US Institute of 3D Printing Technology (USI3DT)

The US Institute of 3D Technology (USI3DT) California, USA, inspired by President Obama’s initiative and “America Makes,” was established to cater to the upcoming demand for professionals in 3D Printing Technology.

The objective of USI3DT is to impart education and training to students and professionals from various industries to maintain pace with future technology.

USI3DT is a Silicon Valley, California-based education Institute that works for the skill up-gradation of Students, Startups, Researchers, Professionals, and the Industrial Workforce in Emerging and Revolutionary 3D Printing Technology.

USI3DT is India’s first America-based dedicated Institute for learning 3D Printing Technology.
